Off-Canvas Modal Panel

This modal panel will transition in from the right side, and cover half the screen. For mobile screens it will cover all but 60px.

Because the modal panel is full height, scrolling the inner content will feel natural for users, when there is need for a scrollbar. It is also a great pattern for responsive designs.
